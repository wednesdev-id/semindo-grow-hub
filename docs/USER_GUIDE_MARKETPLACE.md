# Marketplace Search & Filter - User Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how to use the advanced search and filter features in the Semindo Grow Hub marketplace for different user roles.

---

## For UMKM Buyers

### Browsing Products

1. **Navigate to Marketplace**
   - Click "Marketplace" in the main navigation
   - You'll see featured products and categories

2. **Using Search**
   - Enter keywords in the search bar at the top
   - Results update automatically as you type

3. **Applying Filters**
   - Click the "Filter" button to open the filter panel
   - **Category**: Select from available categories (Craft, Fashion, Culinary, etc.)
   - **Price Range**: Adjust the slider to set min/max price
   - **Stock Status**: Filter by availability (In Stock, Low Stock, Out of Stock)
   - Click "Apply Filters" to see results

4. **Sorting Results**
   - Use the sort dropdown to order by:
     - Price (Low to High / High to Low)
     - Newest First
     - Most Popular

5. **Pagination**
   - Navigate through pages using the pagination controls at the bottom
   - Adjust items per page if needed

---

## For UMKM Sellers

### Managing Your Products

1. **Access Seller Dashboard**
   - Navigate to "Dashboard" → "Seller Dashboard"
   - View your product list in the "My Products" section

2. **Search Your Products**
   - Use the search bar to find specific products quickly
   - Search works on product titles

3. **Filter Your Products**
   - **Status**: Filter by Active, Draft, or Archived
   - **Category**: View products by category
   - **Stock Status**: Find products that need restocking
   - **Price Range**: Filter by price range

4. **Sort Your Products**
   - Sort by newest, price, or stock level
   - Helps prioritize which products to update

---

## For Admins

### Monitoring All Products

1. **Access Admin Dashboard**
   - Navigate to "Dashboard" → "Admin Dashboard"
   - Go to "Marketplace Management" section

2. **View All Products**
   - See products from all sellers
   - Includes drafts and unpublished items

3. **Advanced Filters**
   - **Search**: Find products by name
   - **Seller**: Filter by specific seller
   - **Status**: View Active, Draft, or Archived products
   - **Category**: Filter by product category

4. **Seller Information**
   - Each product shows the seller name and store
   - Click to view seller details

---

## For Consultants

### Viewing Client Products

1. **Access Consultant Dashboard**
   - Navigate to "Dashboard" → "Consultant Dashboard"
   - Switch to "Marketplace" mode
   - Click "Client Products" tab

2. **Filter Client Products**
   - **Search**: Find specific products
   - **Status**: Filter by Active, Draft, or Inactive
   - View products from all your assigned clients

3. **Helping Clients**
   - Identify products that need improvement
   - Find draft products that could be published
   - Monitor client product performance

---

## For Partners

### Finding Export Opportunities

1. **Access Partner Dashboard**
   - Navigate to "Dashboard" → "Partner Dashboard"
   - View "Export Opportunities" tab

2. **Filter Export-Ready Products**
   - **Region**: Filter by city/region
   - **Category**: Focus on specific product types
   - Products shown meet export-ready criteria

3. **Evaluating Opportunities**
   - View estimated FOB prices
   - See product details and seller information
   - Click "View Details" for more information

---

## For Banks

### Identifying Financing Candidates

1. **Access Bank Dashboard**
   - Navigate to "Dashboard" → "Bank Dashboard"
   - View "Financing Candidates" tab

2. **Filter Candidates**
   - **Location**: Search by city
   - **Minimum Revenue**: Set revenue threshold
   - View stores sorted by performance

3. **Candidate Information**
   - See estimated revenue and order count
   - View eligibility status
   - Click "Offer Financing" to initiate process

## Tips for Best Results

### Search Tips
- Use specific keywords for better results
- Try different spellings or related terms
- Combine search with filters for precision

### Filter Tips
- Start broad, then narrow down
- Reset filters if you get no results
- Save common filter combinations (coming soon)

### Performance Tips
- Filters are applied server-side for fast results
- Pagination helps load large result sets quickly
- Use sort options to prioritize what matters most

## Troubleshooting

### No Results Found
- Check your spelling
- Try removing some filters
- Broaden your price range
- Select "All Categories"

### Slow Loading
- Check your internet connection
- Try reducing the number of items per page
- Clear browser cache if persistent

### Filter Not Working
- Ensure you clicked "Apply Filters"
- Try refreshing the page
- Check if you're logged in (for protected features)
